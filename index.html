Culture & Nature

Traditions and Festivals in

Gania is rich in culture and traditions, with various festivals celebrated throughout the year.

Sri Raghunath Jew Temple is very old and famous temple which is near of Gania marcket. In the heart of our peaceful village lies the Shri Ram Temple, a sacred place that brings together tradition, devotion, and community spirit. Surrounded by lush greenery and simple village homes, the temple stands as a symbol of faith and cultural heritage.The temple is dedicated to Lord Ram, the embodiment of righteousness and virtue in Hindu mythology. Every morning, the sound of temple bells and chants of "Ram Naam" echo through the air, bringing a sense of calm and devotion to the villagers.

Lankapodi festival is very famous festival in this village.Lankapodi Jatra is a unique festival that reenacts the burning of Lanka, a significant event from the Hindu epic Ramayana. As the spectators revel in the joy of this ancient tradition, the Lankapodi Yatra continues to thrive, preserving Odisha‚Äôs cultural heritage for generations to come. With its rich history, vibrant performances, and enthusiastic crowd, this festival is truly a spectacle to behold.

Education

Schools and Literacy in Gania

KISHORE CHANDRA BIDYAPITHA was established in 1958 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in GANIA block of NAYAGARH district of Orissa. The school consists of Grades from 8 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Odia is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

The school has Government building. It has got 1 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 1 boys toilet and it is functional. and 1 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 2000 books in its library. The school does not need ramp for disabled children to access classrooms.
