KISHORE CHANDRA BIDYAPITHA was established in 1958 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in GANIA block of NAYAGARH district of Orissa. The school consists of Grades from 8 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Odia is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April. The school has Government building. It has got 1 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has have electric connection. The source of Drinking Water in the school is Tap Water and it is functional. The school has 1 boys toilet and it is functional.
